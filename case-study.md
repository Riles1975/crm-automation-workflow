# AI CRM Automation Workflow Case Study

## Objective

Build an automated CRM workflow that reduces manual administration, improves lead quality, and ensures consistent follow-up execution.

## Challenge

Many organizations rely on manual CRM updates, causing:

- Incomplete records
- Missed follow-ups
- Poor visibility
- Inefficient sales operations

## Solution

An AI-powered workflow was designed using n8n and OpenAI.

The system:

1. Captures new leads
2. Enriches lead records
3. Scores and qualifies opportunities
4. Creates CRM records automatically
5. Assigns pipeline stages
6. Generates follow-up tasks
7. Produces automated reporting

## Workflow Stages

### Lead Capture

Collects lead information from forms, APIs, or webhooks.

### Contact Enrichment

Enhances records with additional company and contact data.

### Lead Qualification

Uses business rules and AI scoring to prioritize opportunities.

### CRM Record Creation

Automatically creates or updates CRM records.

### Pipeline Assignment

Routes leads into appropriate sales stages.

### Follow-Up Automation

Creates tasks and reminders for sales teams.

### Reporting

Provides visibility into sales activity and pipeline performance.

## Technology Stack

- n8n
- OpenAI
- APIs
- Webhooks
- CRM Platforms
- JSON

## Business Impact

Potential benefits include:

- Reduced administrative workload
- Faster lead response times
- Improved CRM accuracy
- Better pipeline visibility
- Increased sales efficiency

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how workflow automation and AI can modernize CRM operations while reducing manual effort.
